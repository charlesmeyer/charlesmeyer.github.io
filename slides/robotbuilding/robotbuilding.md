##Robotics Challenge#

* Navigate to light emitting beacon
* Avoid or traverse various obstacles
* Team that completes each course in the least time wins

</section>

##Requirements#

* Fully autonomous robot
* Max height 15cm
* Less than 0.5 Kg
* Safe
